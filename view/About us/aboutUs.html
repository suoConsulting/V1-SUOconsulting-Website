About Us

What We Are



SUO Consulting is providing solutions relating to business purpose and strategy consultancy, cross-border trade, investments and commercial transactions for business, Corporate and Debt Restructuring, Mergers and Acquisitions, Project and Contract Management Consultancy, Human Resource Mobility and Immigration, Start-up Advisory and International Business with related issues of IPR and data management, international taxation, Anti-bribery Investigations.

What We Do



We provide customer focused, tailor-made solutions to assist their professional and portfolio growth. We are nothing if not for our relationships with our loyal clientele. It is what differentiates us from the rest - a human touch. We have dedicated professionals that give their undivided attention to each of our clients, carefully articulating their business acumen, experience and preferences, identifying the options that fit them best and guide them in its execution.

Our Team

PURPOSE



We are an organisation that is impact-driven and relationship-centric. What allows us to stand tall in the market today, is our vast and strong network throughout the world. It is a lattice of prestigious law firms, business leaders, incubators & accelerators, government officials, chambers of commerce, international investment bodies, immigration consultants and mobility professionals.

VALUES



Our client-first principles, ethos of clear-cut legal compliance and commitment to transparency underlie all of our advisory, outreach and decision-making. We make no compromises in ensuring that all 3 dimensions are firmly in place at each and every step.
